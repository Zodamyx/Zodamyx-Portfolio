About Me

Zodamyx is a digital artist with an AAS (associate of applied science) degree in Digital Media Arts. She has knowledge in various mediums such as photography, animation, traditional drawing, digital art, and logo design. Her specialty is in digital illustration and character design. She enjoys creating concepts and designs for characters along with their lore and cultural background. She can work with a variety of art styles, experimenting with different ones, whether they are simplistic or complex. She has some pieces containing realism, but she mainly works with surrealism.
