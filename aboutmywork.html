About My Work

My work consists of monsters and strange creatures with designs based on myths and folklore. Some designs are inspired by characters from video games and shows. Others are just random characters that either manifested in my imagination or from small doodles. I focus more on making original characters, so there isn't a lot of fanart. Sometimes, I like to design fictional logos and business ideas that would fit into the world my characters live in.
